About Crimson Crown

Building the Future of South Asia Esports

Our Story

Crimson Crown was founded to fill the gap in South Asia’s esports ecosystem. While regions like NA, EU, and SEA had organized scrims and tournaments, South Asia lacked a structured platform. Our mission is to change that.

Mission

To create regular scrims, expand into structured tournaments, and eventually partner with publishers to bring official esports to South Asia.

Vision

Crimson Crown is not just about matches. It’s about proving that structured esports is possible here and building a system where players, teams, and creators can thrive.
