Who We Are

Building the future of competitive gaming in South Asia

Crimson Crown was founded with one simple vision — to bring professionalism and structure to the esports scene in South Asia. While other regions had active tournament organizers, our region lacked consistent platforms for competitive players. That's why we took the first step — hosting weekly scrims and tournaments for Farlight 84, building a strong community of players, and paving the way for official esports recognition.
