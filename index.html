About Crimson Crown

Crimson Crown is a South Asia based esports organization hosting weekly scrims, clan competitions, and tournaments. Our mission is to create a professional stage for rising talent in competitive gaming.

We organize tournaments, highlight top-performing clans, and build a strong community across platforms. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive gamer, Crimson Crown has a place for you.
